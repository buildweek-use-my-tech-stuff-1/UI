Frequently Asked Questions

I'm thinking of renting out my equipment, is it safe?

Yes! The renter is responsible for all lost/damaged equipment.

I'm considering renting, how long does it take to receive the equipment?

It depends on method of acquisition. In most cases, we pair you with local rentals if possible. Some people will ship their equipment for an additional cost.

I need to keep the equipment longer than expected. What do I do?

You can easily pay for an extension on the rental agreement. See your account info for details.

What happens if the equipment I rented Arrives defective?

If you receive equipment and it does not work as described, put in a claim with our support staff immediately and provide details.

What if I want to get rid of the equipment I'm renting out?

You may post your Item as rent to own, and the renter can make payments until the entire amount is fulfilled. Alternatively we may buy it from you if it is in great condition.

What if the equipment I'm renting gets damanged to no fault of my own?

As per the rental agreement you assume full responsibility. We do offer partial coverage insurance at an additional cost.
